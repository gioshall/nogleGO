Our People

Nogle is a group of professional individuals committed to building a radically different approach to software design, development and management. Thinking critically and disruptively to help our clients achieve their goals and help them succeed.

Through experimentation and innovation, we champion software excellence to revolutionize the information technology industry, use technology to help our customers have a competitive advantage and extract more out of existing systems.

We bring together the most capable, driven and passionate people to analyse business models, apply innovative technological solutions and achieve sustainable business models by having an integral and cohesive approach between software design, development and management; hardware; and business goals.

At Nogle we have created teams specialized in different areas of hardware and software design, development and delivery. These teams independently work on all projects along with other teams under the supervision of the project leaders. This ensures the most specialized development in all aspects of the technology for each of our projects.

The Mission

Never stop improving. Allow for inspiration and innovation. Learn from each other. The Nogle vision is one that keeps learning, innovating, and never stops improving to always excel.

Utilizing technology and forward-thinking to continuously innovate, challenge traditional business models, be disruptive and empower our customers in becoming global leaders in their respective industries.

  • TO OUR CUSTOMERS

    Delivering innovative, specialized solutions to address specific challenges, enable competitive advantages and/or efficiency, in consideration to sustainable and economically sound business models.

  • TO OUR SHAREHOLDERS

    Of course to maximize profit, optimize productivity, but also to create a unique company filled with passionate and happy individuals, loyal and committed to our success and our customer’s success.

  • TO OUR STAFF

    Foster the areas of each person’s excellence, inspire and motivate passion, prosperity and a healthy work-life balance, where every single person contributes to our success and the Nogle vision.

The Nogle Values

  • 1
    Honesty & Respectful

    You are sincere and thoughtful to both yourself and your colleagues.

    You are non-political when you disagree with others.

    You admit to your mistakes and shortcomings and strive to improve on them.

  • 2
    Curious and Inquisitive

    You are curious and able to learn quickly and eagerly.

    You seek knowledge and understanding of how important your role is in what we are doing, how impactful it is to the market.

  • 3
    Grit

    You will do whatever it takes to complete the task, the ability to persevere.

    You are positive, optimistic and confident.

  • 4
    Passion

    You have the desire to see the team succeed.

    You believe in what you do and in your teammates.

    You inspire others with your aspiration.

  • 5
    Good Judgement

    You consider all facts before making wise decisions.

    You are able to identify articulately the root cause of any problem and formulate a solution to it.

    You identify articulately what you are and are not, when getting things done.
