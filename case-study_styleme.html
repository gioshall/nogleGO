A 3D virtual-fitting platform for online retailers that allows customers to try-on, match, and catalogue their wardrobe from the comfort of their home.

Visit Style.me

Giving customers the ability to try on what they want, where they want, when they want.

A new dimension in online shopping is emerging, and interactive technology solutions are becoming its key theme. With customers the dictating force of an ever-sophisticated shopping generation, the demands of delivering elements of the physical to the virtual world is one of the many driving forces in outside-the-box thinking.

Nogle’s challenge was to create an innovative technology solution focused on bringing the novelty and “fun” typically found in offline shopping to the online community. Our team of brilliant developers and business minds came to together to create Style Me….. a creative, software-driven, full-stack solution centered on the ability to style yourself in a virtual fitting room.

Through Style Me, shoppers can enter the virtual dressing room, create their 3D avatar, and try on clothing.

Virtual clothing is created in 3D as an exact match to the physical clothing and dressed on their avatars using a complex physics-based simulation engine that accounts for the individual clothing specs and fabric properties. The platform also suggests to shoppers what size fits them best accordingly to their body shape and size.

For the fashion industry

Style Me provides a back-end dashboard with real-time analytics on millions of data points in relation to their customer’s body shapes and sizes, their products’ performance, and the interaction between them thus allowing them to better plan their marketing strategies and up-sell their products.

Through this collaboration, Nogle has proven how technology and fashion can collaborate in seamless harmony and, with the focus on customer value at the center, provide the ultimate customer experience.
