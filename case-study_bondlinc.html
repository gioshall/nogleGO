Mobile trading platform for corporate and sovereign bonds designed for private banking customers.

Visit BONDLINC

Private banks (PB) are always looking for ways to create more value for their clients and increase their assets under management through innovative offerings. Currently, there is no mobile app for private clients to trade bonds. Bonds are a core holding for most private banking clients. Furthermore, there is no organized way for private banks to convey their range of products to clients in a user-friendly manner.

BONDLINC is the solution to both those problems and will become the new standard for private banking.

BONDLINC partnered with Nogle to develop such a solution. A new world in the private banking is emerging, and partnerships are becoming its key theme as BONDLINC provided market knowledge while Nogle provided the technology solutions.

The system demands stability and high quality – such that BONDLINC can offer both clients and customers smooth business process, swift realization and a quick release to the market. Quick, short cycle iterations ensured the project ran within the time frame allocated. Working in a truly Agile manner, the team collaborated closely – co-locating, daily stand ups, continuous integration and automated testing were key to their success.

The results? BONDLINC has presented their new business innovation to private banks and landed key industry clients.

Nogle has amazing processes and methodology that accelerate the learning curve and the most impressive one is how they manage the cost so efficiently.
