Computer Graphics Engineer - Cloth Simulation Engine

Job Description

  • C/C++ code development of physical cloth simulation algorithm.
  • Automate the process of cloth simulation from garment to final virtual fitting result.
  • GPU algorithm acceleration
  • Documentation, version control, and maintenance of developed codes
  • Integrate developed modules with other modules in the company

Core Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree in Computer Science / Engineering / Mathematics with 3+ years of C/C++ development experience or a Master's degree (or higher) in Computer Science / Engineering / Mathematics with 1+ years of C/C++ development experience other related technical discipline.
  • Background of computer graphics algorithms, including geometry processing, physical simulation, animation, CUDA ...etc
  • Open-minded and willing to share and discuss with team members

Desired Qualifications

  • Experience in windows application development would be a plus
