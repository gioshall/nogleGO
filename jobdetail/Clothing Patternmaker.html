Clothing Patternmaker

We are looking for clothing pattern makers to join our digital clothing development team. This team specializes in creation and development of 3D virtual garments for the fashion industry. This patternmaking role requires an individual who has worked with highly detailed patterns as well as having an excellent eye for detail. Product patternmaking experience essential.

Job Description

  • Designing and constructing accurate patterns
  • Creating detailed patterns on computer programs such as Gerber or Lectra
  • Pattern grading and technical specifications

Core Requirements

  • Bachelor's degree or minimum of 4-5 years experience in patternmaking
  • Qualifications in design/garment construction
  • Excellent knowledge of patternmaking, grading, fitting, and marker making techniques
  • Understanding how to construct garments from patterns
  • Knowledge of different fabrics
  • Knowledge of body shapes
  • Experienced using CAD/CAM, adobe illustrator, and web based Product Development software
  • Experienced using MS Office Programs, including Excel and Word
  • Must be team oriented
