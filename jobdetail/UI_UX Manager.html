UI/UX Manager

We are looking for a highly creative and passionate UI/UX Manager with proven ability to lead and inspire a design team to deliver top-notch experiences. He/she will be responsible for establishing User Interface/User Experience design standards, guidelines and strategies that serve as the foundation for creating cohesive experiences as well as designing impactful, usable and accessible web and mobile sites. A candidate with strong communication and collaboration skills and experience working with all levels of management in obtaining consensus on project requirements and deliverables is a must.

Job Description

  • Direct UI/UX across multiple projects at various points of the production pipeline
  • Manage multiple projects and ensure that all designs are interpreted and implemented as specified and meet all business requirements
  • Distill complex interactions into clear, understandable design solutions and clearly rationalize your strategic thinking to stakeholders
  • Be a user centric design advocate to lead discussions s on topics including visual design, interaction design, information architecture, and user research"

Core Requirements

  • Bachelor's Degree in Human Factors, Interaction, Graphic or Interface Design. Master’s Degree a plus
  • 7+ years of experience in User Interface or User Experience for complex online systems and mobile platforms, and at least 2-3 years in a leadership role
  • Experience implementing A/B testing plans to analyze and improve conversion rates and usability
  • Proven experience creating detailed wireframes, site outlines, process flows, and navigation models in a thorough, clean, and concise way using industry standard tools
  • Fluency in best practices for mobile and web-based design, as well as strong knowledge of information architecture
  • Stay current with latest design and technology trends across web and mobile platforms, and adopt modern techniques as appropriate
  • Strong verbal and visual communication, written communication, interpersonal and presentation skills
  • Proficiency in HTML, CSS, JavaScript and RWD
