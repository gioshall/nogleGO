Our People Our People Our People Our People

Nogle is group of professional individuals committed to build a radically different approach to software design, development and management. Thinking critically and disruptively to help our clients achieve their goals and help them succeed. Through experimentation and innovation, we champion software excellence to revolutionise the information technology industry, use technology to help our customers have a competitive advantage and extract more out of existing systems. We bring together the most capable, driven and passionate people to analyse business models, apply innovative technological solutions and achieve sustainable business models by having an integral and cohesive approach between software design, development and management; hardware; and business goals. At Nogle we have created teams specialised in different areas of hardware and software design, development and delivery. These teams independently work on all projects along with other teams under the supervision of the project leaders. This ensure the most specialised development in all aspects of the technology for each of our projects.
